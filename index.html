Our Name & Story

Why “Moon”?

Moon is my first rescued cat. I found him on the street and brought him home in November 2024. He is an orange male cat, and he is the reason this organization exists.

His name became the name of this mission — because every stray out there deserves what Moon has now: a name, a home, and someone who cares.

🌙
Nuzhat Nousin Seeme
Founder · MoonMeow · Moulvibazar, Sylhet
What We Do

Pillars of Care

MoonMeow is just beginning its journey. Here is what we are working on.

🍲

Daily Feeding

No cat should go to bed hungry. We feed stray cats in our area regularly — making sure familiar faces on the street have a warm meal each day.

💉

Medical Care

Vaccinations, basic treatment, and care for cats in need. We started with our own cats and are working to extend this to more strays as we grow.

💛

Fundraising

Every taka helps us buy food, cover vet costs, and build shelters. We raise funds from kind people who believe stray cats deserve better.

🏠

Adoption

We connect rescued, socialized stray cats with loving families in Moulvibazar — giving them a forever home where they truly belong.
