Our Name & Story

Why "Moon"?

Moon is my first rescued cat. I found him on the street and brought him home in November 2024. He is an orange male cat, and he is the reason this organization exists.

His name became the name of this mission — because every stray out there deserves what Moon has now: a name, a home, and someone who cares.

🌙
Nuzhat Nousin Seeme
Founder · MoonMeow · Moulvibazar, Sylhet
What We Do

Pillars of Care

MoonMeow is just beginning its journey. For now, we focus on two things that matter most — keeping strays fed, and keeping them healthy.

🍲

Daily Feeding

No cat should go to bed hungry. We feed stray cats in our area regularly — making sure familiar faces on the street have a warm meal waiting for them each day.

💉

Medical Care

Vaccinations, basic treatment, and care for cats in need. We started with our own cats and we are working to extend this to more strays in Moulvibazar as we grow.

Meet the Founder

Nuzhat Nousin Seeme

Founder & Director · MoonMeow.com · Moulvibazar, Sylhet

Nuzhat Nousin Seeme is the founder of MoonMeow — Moulvibazar's first organization dedicated to the welfare of stray cats. Her journey began not with a plan, but with a cat. In November 2024, she rescued an orange stray she named Moon, and everything changed.

Looking at the streets of Moulvibazar, she realized there was no one doing this work — no NGO, no shelter, no one feeding strays consistently. So she decided to start something herself, from zero, with nothing but care and quiet determination.

MoonMeow is still in its early days. But it is real, it is growing, and it started because one person refused to look away.

Animal Welfare Advocate Cat Rescuer Moulvibazar, Sylhet
